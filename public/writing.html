Article published in Offscreen magazine, issue 9

The written word has been a personal fascination since my childhood days, when I would often get lost in writing and illustrating my own books.

My writing today is largely of a personal nature, having shifted somewhat after spending several years authoring articles on design, culture, and occasionally, socio-political issues. Lately, I’ve been entertaining thoughts of dipping my toes into the world of short-form fiction (though nothing has materialised of this — yet).

Most of my ongoing work can be found on my blog, though I’ve also been fortunate to have my articles published by other people, and even printed.

Recent and ongoing writing

A blog by Cole Peters
I have been publishing articles to my blog consistently for the past 2–3 years. In 2012, I maintained a schedule of publishing one article per day for several months. More recently, I have decided to get back into a regular flow of personal writing. New articles are published every week, on Sundays, and are also syndicated to Medium.
Published writing
My writing has been published in print by Offscreen magazine (see issue 9). One of my blog posts was republished by Adbusters in 2014. A bite-sized opinion piece can be found on DesignWeek’s VoxPop (free registration required to read).
