Cole Peters

— photographer.

Sclater Street, East London; 2015

Ever since my father put an Olympus Stylus Epic into my then-adolescent hands, I have been a devout practitioner of photography.

I am a street and documentary photographer at heart, driven to capture both pseudo-fictional stories and factual events. I shoot strictly in black and white, a practice held dear from my days of developing and printing my own images in a home darkroom.

I have spent nearly every weekend of 2015 on the streets of London and other locations in Europe, capturing spontaneous moments in an ongoing series of images which seek to engage the viewer in the creation of spontaneous narratives.

A book of photographs is currently being developed for self-publication 2016.
