ΔS 2020

∆S (‘delta S’) is a series of seven paintings motivated by a fascination with entropy — in short, the tendency of systems, from molecules to universes, to evolve from highly ordered states to disordered states of equilibrium. The work is compositionally informed by studies of surfaces in transition towards ‘lessness’.

  • ΔS₁

    Acrylic, pumice, black gesso, India ink on birch
    2 panels, each 10 × 10 in (25 × 25 cm)

  • ΔS₂

    Acrylic, black gesso, paper, India ink, charcoal on birch
    2 panels, each 10 × 10 in (25 × 25 cm)

  • ΔS₃

    Acrylic, pumice, black gesso on birch
    3 panels, 6 × 12 in, 9 × 12 in, 10 × 12 in
    (15 × 30 cm, 22 × 30 cm, 25 × 30 cm)

  • ΔS₄

    Acrylic, iridescent acrylic, pumice, black gesso on birch
    12 × 18 in (30 × 45 cm)

  • ΔS₅

    Acrylic, pumice, black gesso, iridescent acrylic on birch
    30 × 68 in (76 × 172 cm)

  • ΔS₆

    Acrylic, gesso, pumice, spackling, India ink on birch
    16 × 16 in (40 × 40 cm)

  • ΔS₇ (Least Resistance)

    Acrylic, pumice, India Ink, gesso, iridescent acrylic, spackling on birch
    36 × 48 in (122 × 91 cm)
